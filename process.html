How We Work

Our Process

Simple, transparent, and efficient credentialing workflow

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your practice needs, specialty, and target insurance panels. Free assessment of your current credentialing status. No obligation, just honest advice.

2

Document Collection

We provide a detailed checklist and guide you through gathering required documents: licenses, diplomas, CV, malpractice insurance, and more.

3

Application Submission

Our expert team completes and submits all credentialing applications, CAQH profiles, and insurance enrollment forms with precision and accuracy.

4

Follow-up & Approval

We track applications, follow up with payers weekly, and notify you immediately upon approval. Ongoing maintenance included.

5

Ongoing Support

Quarterly CAQH attestations, revalidation reminders, and adding new insurance panels as your practice grows.
